Who We Are

Lifeline for Inclusion and Empowerment (LIFE) is a women-led NGO established in 2017 to serve vulnerable communities in Somalia.

Our Vision & Mission

To promote Somali women's participation in leadership, economic growth and policy decision-making.

Core Values

Integrity, Non-discrimination, Impartiality, Transparency & Accountability.

Who We Serve

Women, children, youth, IDPs, returnees and vulnerable minorities.

Our Partners

We collaborate with trusted partners to maximize impact and reach more communities across Somalia.
