WASH

LIFE Somalia works to improve access to clean water, sanitation, and hygiene services in vulnerable communities. Through the construction of boreholes, wells, and latrines, we ensure families have access to safe water and improved sanitation facilities.

Our hygiene promotion campaigns help communities adopt safe practices, reducing the spread of waterborne diseases and improving overall health. By integrating WASH interventions into schools, health facilities, and households, we create sustainable solutions that support long-term well-being.
