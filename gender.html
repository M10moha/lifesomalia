LIFE Somalia is committed to advancing gender equality by creating safe spaces where women and girls can access resources, support services, and opportunities for leadership. Our programs focus on raising awareness about harmful practices, promoting education, and strengthening protection mechanisms to address gender-based violence in all its forms.

We also work with men, boys, and community leaders to foster positive change and ensure that equality is embraced at every level of society. From advocating against female genital mutilation to supporting survivors of violence with counseling and legal aid, we strive to build inclusive communities where every individual can live with dignity, respect, and opportunity.
