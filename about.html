About LIFE Somalia

Lifeline for Inclusion and Empowerment (LIFE) is a women-led NGO focused on humanitarian response and sustainable development.

Executive Summary

Lifeline for Inclusion and Empowerment (LIFE) is a women-led, non-profit, non-governmental, and non-partisan organization dedicated to fostering a resilient, equitable, and inclusive Somalia. Founded in 2017 by Somali diaspora women in Alberta, Canada, LIFE emerged in response to the devastating drought and humanitarian crisis that threatened millions. Since then, LIFE has been committed to providing immediate humanitarian assistance while addressing long-term challenges such as gender inequality, gender-based violence, and social exclusion. Our mission is to empower women, children, youth, and persons with disabilities through critical services, advocacy, and sustainable development initiatives.

Who We Are

Full Name: LIFE Somalia
Acronym: LIFE
Slogan: Lifeline for Inclusion and Empowerment
Type: Non-Governmental Organization (NGO)
Headquarters: Mogadishu, Somalia
Coverage: Banadir, Southwest State, Hirshabelle, Galmudug, Jubaland, and Puntland.

Vision

An environment where women are free from all forms of inequalities, violence, and harmful practices, and are active participants in leadership, economic growth, and social sectors, with their contributions to society fully realized.

Mission

To promote Somali women’s participation in leadership, economic growth, and policy decision-making. We provide capacity-building and empowerment programs to enable women to contribute significantly to society and play a major role in leadership, entrepreneurship, and politics, while maintaining accountability to all stakeholders.

Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Non-Discrimination
  • Impartiality
  • Transparency & Accountability
