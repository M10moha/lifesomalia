Health

LIFE Somalia delivers essential health services including primary care, maternal and child health, and nutrition support to reduce preventable illnesses and strengthen community resilience. Our mobile clinics and outreach programs ensure that even the most remote populations can access life-saving medical care.

Beyond emergency health services, we also focus on building long-term systems by training health workers, providing vital medical supplies, and supporting disease prevention campaigns. By working hand in hand with communities, we strive to create healthier futures for families across Somalia.
